Information Technology Infrastructure Library

ITIL : The Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) is a set of detailed practices 
for IT activities such as IT service management and IT asset management that focus on aligning 
IT services with the needs of business.     


What is Information Technology Service Management?
Information technology service management (ITSM) is the activities that are performed by an 
organization to design, build, deliver, operate and control information technology (IT) 
services offered to customers.
